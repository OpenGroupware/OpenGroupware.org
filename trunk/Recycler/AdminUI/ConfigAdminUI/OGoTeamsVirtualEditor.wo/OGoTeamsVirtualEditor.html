<#Font> This configuration creates a Postfix virtual file containing the email mappings specified in the team records. It will map a team name to the email addresses of the team members and will also produce virtual aliases for a team if told to do so.

Note: Remember that special chars like umlauts or spaces will be transformed to produce RFC822 compliant email addresses. Take care not to create duplicate email aliases.

<#ACell><#Font>Settings: <#VCell><#EditFont> <#IgnoreExport/> Ignore during export.
<#IgnoreVAddrs/> Ignore virtual addresses attached to team.
<#ACell><#Font>EMails: <#VCell><#EditFont> <#GenEMail/> generate valias for team email address.
